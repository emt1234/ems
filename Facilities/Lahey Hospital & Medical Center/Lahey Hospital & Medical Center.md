The **Lahey Hospital & Medical Center** (formerly known as the **Lahey Clinic**) is an Adult [[Level I Trauma Center]][^about][^dph-trauma], part of the [[Beth Israel Lahey Health]] system, located in Burlington, Massachusetts.
