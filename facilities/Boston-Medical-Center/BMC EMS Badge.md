Grants access to virtually all doors at [Boston Medical Center](./index.md).

## Obtaining
Badges can be obtained from the [BMC Identification Office](./BMC%20Identification%20Office.md).

You will need:
- Proof of EMS employment (uniform/badge should count)
- A photo ID

## Replacing IDs
IDs may be replaced, at a cost, at the [BMC Identification Office](./BMC%20Identification%20Office.md).
