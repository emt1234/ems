## Directions
1. Use [BMC ER Entrance](../BMC%20ER%20Entrance.md)
2. Proceed past triage. **Stop BEFORE you enter Trauma Bays.**
3. Turn left towards [ER Side B](./BMC%20ER%20Side%20B.md), proceed into Side B. (Use [Badge](../BMC%20EMS%20Badge.md) reader on right wall)
4. Proceed straight through Side B, sign directly ahead for "ER Side A"
5. Use Handicap button on left-hand side before reaching Side A doors

> [!NOTE]- Alternative Access via [BMC Non-Emergent Ambulance Entrance](../BMC%20Non-Emergent%20Ambulance%20Entrance.md)
> Alternatively, access via the non-emergency entrance. Follow [directions to enter via non-emergency entrance](../BMC%20Non-Emergent%20Ambulance%20Entrance.md#Directions), once you cross the skybridge use a [badge](../BMC%20EMS%20Badge.md) to enter the elevator immediately on your left, take to floor 1 (directly into [ER Side B](./BMC%20ER%20Side%20B.md)). Walk down hallway, turn right to face ER Side A doors.
